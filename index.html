Countries Human Development Index

This map displays spatial-temporal data which is seen as the HDI for each Nation. Spatial-temporal data consists of spatial data over time; as seen in this map, it shows the HDI of Nations during 2000 and 2018. The Human Development Index looks at a way Nations develop without looking solely at the economy. It includes a life expectancy index, an education index, and a GNI index. All these contribute to a better way to seek the development of the Nation and gives a context where policies can apply to increase further development.
